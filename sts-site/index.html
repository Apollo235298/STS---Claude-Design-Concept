Who We Are

Advanced surgical care — delivered to your operatories.

Southern Travel Surgery was founded after seeing the same thing happen practice after practice — patients scheduled out for months, unable to get access to the specialty care they needed, and wanting to stay with the office they already trust.

STS brings the surgery to them: efficient care, minimal follow-ups, and patients who don't hurt and don't get infected.

Frequently

Questions

Most referring offices have the same five questions. Here's how we answer them.

  • Most partner offices book us one full day per month, scaling up to weekly as case volume grows. We block the day on your schedule; you fill it with the surgical patients you've been referring out. We arrive the morning of, set up in an operatory you designate, and leave the room as we found it.

    Cancellations and add-ons are handled directly with our coordinator the week of, so your front office isn't juggling a third party.

  • You diagnose and treatment plan the way you always have. We review every case in advance with you — typically a 10-minute screen-share the week prior — and confirm CDT codes, sedation type, and any imaging we need.

  • One operatory, a chair, and an assistant for handoffs. That's it. We bring our own sterile instruments, surgical handpieces, monitoring, emergency equipment, and IV setup. We are credentialed, licensed, and fully insured in every state we serve.

  • The patient pays your office, the same way they would for any other procedure. STS bills the host practice on a clean monthly statement based on the cases performed. There are no per-patient claims, no insurance entanglement on our end, and no surprise fees for your patients.

  • We do. Every patient gets a 24-hour follow-up call from our team, and a direct line to the surgeon for 14 days post-op. If a patient calls your office, your front desk has a single number to forward to — we take it from there and keep you in the loop.

  • Surgical liability rests with Dr. Bechtel and Southern Travel Surgery — not your practice. We carry our own malpractice and general liability coverage, operate under our own state license in each jurisdiction we serve, and provide proof of insurance and credentialing to every host practice on file.

    Your office is acting as the facility host, not the surgical provider. We're happy to share our policy specifics and walk your office through the arrangement with your own counsel if helpful.

  • Yes. Mobile and traveling specialist arrangements are a long-standing, well-established model in dentistry — used routinely by anesthesiologists, pediatric specialists, and oral surgeons across the country. STS operates under standard host-facility agreements that satisfy state dental board and anti-kickback requirements.

    We provide template agreements vetted by healthcare counsel and are happy to accommodate any review your practice's attorney recommends before onboarding.
